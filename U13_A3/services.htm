Screen replacement

Replacing the screen on Apple devices, like iPhones, can cost between £150 and £400 , depending on the model and whether original parts are used, with newer models like the iPhone 14 Pro being on the higher end. For Samsung and other Android phones, screen replacement typically ranges from £100 to £300 , influenced by factors such as screen type (e.g., AMOLED or LCD) and device complexity.

Battery replacement

Replacing the battery on Apple devices typically costs between £70 and £129, with prices varying based on the iPhone model and whether the service is done by Apple or a third party. For Samsung and other Android phones, battery replacement ranges from £50 to £150, depending on the device's model and battery specifications.

Device settings

Device settings on Apple phones allow users to customize features like display, notifications, and privacy, typically accessible for free unless professional assistance is required, which may cost £20 to £50 at service center. Samsung and Android phones offer similar settings adjustments, with costs for professional help also ranging from £20 to £50, depending on the complexity of the task.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ Button
A mobile data plan allows you to access the internet on your device using your carrier's network (e.g., 4G or 5G). The plan typically includes a specific amount of data (measured in gigabytes) that you can use for activities like browsing, streaming, and social media. Once your data limit is reached, you may incur additional charges or experience reduced speeds, depending on your carrier's policy.
You can check your data usage through your phone's settings or your carrier's mobile app. For example: On iPhones: Go to Settings > Cellular > Cellular Data Usage.
On Android: Go to Settings > Network & Internet > Data Usage.
Alternatively, log in to your carrier's account portal for detailed usage reports.
First, ensure your mobile signal is strong. Restart your device and check if Airplane Mode is disabled. Verify that your SIM card is properly inserted. If the issue persists, contact your carrier to check for network outages or account-related problems.
To unlock your phone, contact your current carrier and request an unlock code. Most carriers provide this service for free if your account is in good standing and your phone is fully paid off. Once unlocked, you can insert a SIM card from a different carrier.
Roaming occurs when your phone connects to a network outside your carrier's coverage area, such as when you're traveling abroad. Charges depend on your plan; some include free or discounted roaming in specific countries, while others may charge per minute, text, or MB of data. Check with your carrier before traveling to avoid unexpected charges.
