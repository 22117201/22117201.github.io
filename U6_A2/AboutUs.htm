About Celtec

Celtec Games is a small independent game development company that creates original games for desktop and mobile platforms. The company focuses on creative ideas, engaging gameplay, and unique visual styles rather than large-scale mainstream productions. Celtec aims to provide enjoyable and memorable gaming experiences for a wide range of players, from casual users to more dedicated gamers. As an independent studio, it values innovation, flexibility, and close connection with its audience, often sharing updates, blogs, and new product information through its website and social media channels.

Our Mission

  • Provide enjoyable experiences for players
  • Develop games for desktop and mobile
  • Listen to community feedback
  • Continuously improve quality
